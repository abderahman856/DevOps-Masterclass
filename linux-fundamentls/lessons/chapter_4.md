**VIM Editor (Important for DevOps)**

**Lesson 10 — Basic VIM**

Open file:

    vim file.txt

Insert mode:

    i

Save & quit:

    :wq

Quit without saving:

    :q!

**Lesson 11 — VIM Navigation**

Move:

    h j k l

Search:

    /text

Delete line:

    dd

Copy:

    yy

Paste:

    p

**Lesson 12 — Advanced VIM**

Undo:

    u

Redo:

    Ctrl+R

Go to line:

    :5

Show numbers:

    :set number

DevOps Use:

  Editing configs
  
  Editing Dockerfiles
  
  Editing Kubernetes YAML
